About LHS

Leader & Innovator

Lalit Hydraulics Systems was established in 2007 . Company is based in Nasik, Maharashtra and its manufacturing facility is equipped with special purpose machines to manufacture components and its assemblies. Hydraulics has vast applications , and to cater to its needs we have experts who can understand and give its solutions . To cater all types of applications , we have strong design facility , proto type manufacturing facility and CNC machining ste up to cater larger volumes as well . We started by first repairing of hydraulic cylinders and manufacturing of honed tubes, then with need and customer demand we are now capable in delivering solutions in use of hydraulic cylinders and power packs !
