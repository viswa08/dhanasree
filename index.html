About Dhanasree Hydraulics & Equipments

WELCOME TO DHANASREE HYDRAULICS & EQUIPMENTS The Over all company's administration is monitored by Mr. S. Sivakarthikvel. The company is having well experience personnel in various fields like Design, Marketing, Production, Quality, Installation & Commissioning services. The company is located in Padi. Industrial Hub of Chennai Metro which is well connected by Air, Sea, Road and Rail. Having started our operations from Chennai. Tamil Nadu, we have achieved huge professional success to become one of the most prestigious manufacturers and suppliers of a broad assembly of Special Purpose Machines. Our product line includes Assembly Automation Systems, Hydraulic Presses. Our sole proprietorship business entity, Dhanasree Hydraulic & Equipments was established in 2012 at Srinivasa Nagar, Padi, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.

The firm is been manufacturing and supplying excellent quality of complete engineering systems and hydraulic power units to the complete satisfaction of the customers.

The company offering in various product ranges are Hydraulic Power Pack, Hydraulic Cylinder, Hydraulic SS Cylinder, Hydraulic Jack Hydraulic Press, Hydraulic Workshop Press, Hydraulic SPM, Hydraulic Scissor Lift, Hydraulic Training Kit, Hydraulic Equipments, Hydrostatic Motorized Water Test Pump, Hydraulic Compact Power Pack, Hydraulic Oil Transferring Pump, Hydraulic Accumulator, Hydraulic Manifold Block, Hydraulic Car Washing Service Lift, Hydraulic Valves & Accessories, Pneumatic Valves & Accessories, Hydraulic Hand Pallet Truck Manual Stacker, Semi Electric Stacker, Barrel Lifter and many more. Being the prominent manufacturer and supplier of broad assembly of Special Purpose Machines. We ensure the precise engineering and accuracy in the products.

Our clients have widely acclaimed the products to the best amongst those available in the market. The proprietor of the firm Mr. Sivakarthikvel is professionally qualified and has a lot of experience in the fields of Marketing, Designing, Production, Quality Control, Commissioning services. He is quite capable to manage the business activities very effectively. We have technically qualified and highly experience workmen employed are extremely dedicated and talented in their respective domain.
